3PL(Third-Party Logistics)

For Third-Party Logistics (3PL) services, S K Translines Pvt. Ltd. offers comprehensive and versatile solutions tailored to the unique needs of clients seeking efficient and reliable logistics support.

We provide Third-Party logistics to other logistic companies and service providers that typically specialize in integrated operations of warehousing and transportation services that can be scaled and customized to customers' needs, based on market conditions, to meet the demands and delivery service requirements for their products. Our services often extend beyond logistics to include value-added services related to the production or procurement of goods, such as services that integrate parts of the supply chain.


  • Cost and time savings
  • Low capital commitment
  • Focus
  • Flexibility
  • Capacity

S. T. Parcel Services:

We are the exclusive licensee of MSRTC (Maharashtra State Road Tourism Corporation) for its Parcel service through 16500 buses across Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Telangana, Chattisgarh, Gujarat, and Rajasthan. For this business, our infrastructure comprises 572 franchisees, operating under the name Uncle Parcels and Forwarders Pvt. Ltd.



At S K Translines Pvt. Ltd., we specialize in providing comprehensive and adaptable Third-Party Logistics (3PL) services tailored to meet the unique requirements of clients seeking reliable, efficient, and scalable logistics support.



Tailored Logistics Solutions

Our 3PL services cater not only to logistic companies but also to various service providers looking for integrated warehousing and transportation solutions. We pride ourselves on offering scalable and customizable services that align with market dynamics, ensuring the fulfillment of product demands and delivery service prerequisites for our clients.



Holistic Supply Chain Integration

Going beyond conventional logistics, our services extend to encompass value-added functionalities integrated into the supply chain. These additional services revolve around enhancing the production or procurement of goods, thereby enriching the overall efficiency of the supply chain ecosystem.
