Operation Executive

In a logistics or transportation organization, an operation executive's responsibilities usually include supervising and managing a range of operational duties and procedures. Depending on the company, the precise duties may differ, but the following is a rough summary of the job description:


Job Description
Role Title: Operation Executive
Location: Jalgaon
Experience: 2 Years
Education: Usually, a bachelor's degree in business administration, supply chain management, logistics,
or a similar discipline is preferred. However, in place of formal schooling, appropriate work experience and
certifications could be taken into account.
Work Experience: It is preferred to have previous expertise in operations, logistics, or a related position within
the transportation sector. It is frequently preferable for candidates to have prior expertise managing
shipments, organizing transportation activities, or interacting with inventory management systems.
Coordination and Execution: • Manage transportation schedules and coordinate with drivers.
• Monitor progress of shipments for smooth workflow
Documentation and Record-Keeping: Maintain accurate records of consignment details, transportation documents, and inventory.
Prepare and update documentation as required.
Inventory Management: • Monitor and manage inventory levels for optimal stock availability.
• Coordinate with warehouse personnel for inventory movements and audits.
Knowledge and Skills: • Strong organizational and time management skills.
• Proficient interpersonal and communication abilities.
• Proficiency in using computer systems and software applications, especially MS Office.
• Knowledge of the scheduling, routing, and documentation procedures involved in logistics and transportation.
• The capacity to work under pressure in a hectic setting and attention to detail.
• Capabilities for making decisions and addressing problems.
• Familiarity with safety rules and standards of compliance.
Self-Motivation and Drive: Proactive and self-driven approach to business development. the capacity to work autonomously and assume
initiative.Perseverance and resilience in seizing new chances.
