F.C.I(Food Corporation of India)

For the Food Corporation of India (FCI), S K Translines Pvt. Ltd. offers specialized logistical support through dedicated warehouse facilities strategically located across key regions in India. With our tailored solutions and expertise, we ensure the secure transportation and efficient handling of food grains and commodities entrusted to us by F.C.I.

Unloading/Loading of foodgrains bags from /into railway wagons, trucks etc., stacking the foodgrains in bags, bagging, weighment, standardization, cleaning of foodgrains, etc., and transporting of foodgrains from Railway Good shed/Siding to Corporation Godowns or vice/versa or transporting them from any place to any other place in & around MSWC & Rail Head


  • Unloading from railway wagons and loading onto trucks or containers.
  • Truck transportation between railway sidings and godowns.
  • Cleaning activities within the logistics process.
  • Regular physical verification of stored goods.
  • Filling gunnies with grains, stitching, stacking, or loading.

Food grain handling and transportation:

In the realm of food grain handling and transportation, S K Translines Pvt. Ltd. takes pride in its significant role as a trusted logistics partner for government clients. Managing an impressive volume of approximately 1,50,000 metric tons per month, we collaborate seamlessly with esteemed organizations like Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporations (MSWC) and Central Warehousing Corporations (CWC). Our commitment goes beyond mere quantity; it encompasses a dedicated focus on efficiency, reliability, and adherence to the highest industry standards.


With a well-coordinated and systematic approach, we ensure the timely and secure transportation of food grains to their designated destinations. Our team employs stringent quality control measures to guarantee that the food grains are handled with the utmost care, maintaining their freshness and integrity throughout the transit process. Whether it's a short-distance journey within the state or transportation to diverse locations, our unwavering commitment to excellence makes us the preferred choice for government entities relying on the seamless handling and transportation of essential food commodities. S K Translines Pvt. Ltd. stands as a beacon of reliability and efficiency in the crucial task of food grain logistics for our esteemed government clients.

Seamless Handling and Transportation

Our specialized services encompass a wide spectrum of logistical activities, including the unloading/loading of foodgrain bags from railway wagons, trucks, and containers. We ensure the safe stacking, bagging, weighment, standardization, cleaning, and transportation of foodgrains from Railway Good Sheds/Siding to Corporation Godowns or vice versa, addressing F.C.I's precise needs.



Comprehensive Logistical Tasks

The intricacies of our services involve multifaceted logistical tasks, ensuring the seamless movement of foodgrains:
Efficient Unloading and Loading: We meticulously unload goods from railway wagons onto trucks or containers, optimizing the loading process for secure transportation.
Truck Transportation: Facilitating seamless transportation between railway sidings and godowns, our fleet of trucks ensures the swift and secure movement of foodgrains.
Cleaning and Verification: We maintain rigorous cleanliness standards within our logistics processes and conduct regular physical verifications to ensure the integrity of stored goods.
Bagging and Stacking: Tasks such as filling gunnies with grains, stitching, stacking, and loading are meticulously managed to ensure organized storage and efficient dispatch.
