Human Resource Management

An HR Executive's job is to assist the human resources department with a range of operational and administrative duties. They may also be referred to as HR Assistants or HR Coordinators. They are essential to maintaining efficient HR operations and helping staff members with HR-related issues. Depending on the company, the precise duties may differ, but the following is a rough summary of the job description:


Job Description
Role Title: Human Resource Management
Location: Jalgaon
Experience: 2 Years
Education: Generally speaking, a bachelor's degree in business administration, human resources management, or
a similar discipline is preferred. HR management credentials that are pertinent can be useful.
Knowledge of HR procedures: Familiarity with HR principles, procedures, and employment rules and regulations. Understanding of
recruitment processes, employee interactions, performance management, and HR administration.
Interpersonal and Communication Skills: • Proficient in both writing and spoken communication.
• Capacity to communicate with staff members at all organizational levels in an efficient manner
• Problem-solving and active listening skills
Attention to Detail: Strong attention to detail and accuracy in data administration and documentation. the capacity to
protect sensitive employee data in confidence.
Computer Skills: Proficiency in MS Office suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and knowledge with HRIS (Human Resources
Information Systems) or HRMS (Human Resources Management Systems) software.
Payroll software experience is a bonus.
Organizational and Time Management Skills: Capacity to manage several deadlines, prioritize projects, and operate in a fast-paced setting.
strong organizing abilities to keep up with documents and records related to HR.
Teamwork and Collaboration: Capacity to cooperate with coworkers and stakeholders as well as work well in a team. Willingness
to help and assist others in the HR department.
Talent Acquisition and Onboarding: • Coordinate recruitment, initial interviews, and onboarding processes.
• Manage candidate records, HR databases, and ensure accurate employee records.
HR Operations and Support: • Assist in policy implementation, address employee queries, and support engagement initiatives.
• Manage HR administrative tasks, including document preparation, payroll, and benefits administration.
