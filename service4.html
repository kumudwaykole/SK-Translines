R.C.F(Rashtriya Chemicals and Fertilizers Ltd)

We provide services to Rashtriya Chemicals & Fertilizers Ltd (R.C.F) for handling & transportation of fertilizers in bags of 50 Kgs., 45 Kgs., 25 Kgs., and / or 10 Kg each etc. and also Minikit bags from JALGAON Rake point / warehouse to various destinations of Jalgaon district and other destinations of Maharashtra State. The Services involves handling and transportation of fertilizers at JALGAON Railhead/Godown to various destinations of Jalgaon district and other destinations of corresponding State in such a way that there will be neither cut and torn bags nor any weight loss due to use of pointed objects / hooks etc,



TRANSPORTATION OF FROM WAREHOUSE

We ensure that the material handed over to us By R.C.F is delivered in full without transshipment at destinations defined to us by R.C.F without any damage either to the material or to the packing in such a way that there will be neither cut and torn bags nor any weight loss within 3 days from the date of lifting from JALGAON rake point / warehouse.
