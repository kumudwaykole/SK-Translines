Our Rail Transportation

We are well-equipped to transport agricultural, FMCG, heavy, and bulky cargo via Indian Railways. With our experience, professionalism, and specialized knowledge in transportation, we offer long-distance logistics services in India through Railways.


We take pride in being among the leading and growing Indian transportation companies, providing complete logistics solutions with a focus on integrated Multimodal Transport Solutions. Our emphasis on continuous upgrades, innovation, and technology implementation ensures safe, efficient, customer-friendly, and swift movement of goods. We offer combined Railways and Road Door-to-Door Logistics services for all customer categories.



EXPERIENCE,TECHNOLOGY & SYSTEM DRIVEN PERFECTION

S K Translines Pvt. Ltd. is a reliable logistics service provider that has developed its own integrated ERP system for Road and Rail transport. Our distinct process mapping for rail dispatches, fully online operations, GPS-enabled fleets, and Railway rakes offer an unparalleled experience to customers.


We adhere to JMP (Journey Management Plan) for every trip, prioritizing safety by prohibiting driving between 1 am to 6 am and training our drivers in defensive driving skills, thereby reducing the likelihood of road accidents and transit breakdowns.


Key Offerings:

  • 24/7 Customer care support.
  • GPS-enabled Trucks.
  • Online tracking of Railway wagons.
  • One-time usable bottom seals for every Container/Railway wagon.

Expertise in Operations

Handling and Transportation of Food Grains:

We handle nearly 1,50,000 Metric Tons monthly for government customers like CWC (Central Warehousing Corporations) and MSWC (Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporations).


Handling and Transportation of Fertilizers:

S K Translines Pvt. Ltd. manages the transportation of fertilizers for Zuari Chemicals and Fertilizers Ltd in the Jalgaon region.
