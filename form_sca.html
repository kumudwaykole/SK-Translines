Supply Cchain Management

The role of a Supply Chain Manager in a logistics or transportation company is to oversee and manage end-to-end supply chain operations. The responsibilities may vary, but here's a general outline of the job description:


Job Description
Role Title: Supply Chain Management
Location: Jalgaon
Experience: Significant experience in supply chain management, logistics, or a related field is usually required.
Strong track record of successfully managing supply chain processes, teams, and achieving targets.
Education: A bachelor's or master's degree in logistics, supply chain management, business administration,
or a related field is typically preferred. Relevant work experience and certifications in supply chain
management are valuable.
Leadership and Management Skills: • Strong leadership and team management abilities.
• Excellent communication, interpersonal, and negotiation skills.
• Ability to motivate and inspire a team to achieve supply chain goals.
Knowledge and Expertise: • In-depth understanding of logistics and transportation processes.
• Familiarity with industry best practices and emerging trends.
• Proficiency in using supply chain management software and tools.
• Knowledge of relevant legal and safety regulations.
Resource Allocation: Manage and allocate resources effectively to meet operational requirements. Oversee manpower,
equipment, and inventory to ensure smooth operations and timely delivery.

To apply for any of the positions listed above, fill out the application form below and attach your resume OR please send your resume to hr@sktranslines.com Our HR team will review your application, and if you meet the qualifications, we will contact you for further steps in the hiring process.
